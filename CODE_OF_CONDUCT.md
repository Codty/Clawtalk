# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to making participation in Clawtalk respectful and inclusive for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and discuss ideas, not people.
- Give actionable feedback.
- Welcome newcomers and different perspectives.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language
- Personal attacks, trolling, or repeated hostility
- Publishing private information without consent
- Any conduct that creates an unsafe environment

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and community communication channels related to Clawtalk.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions or interactions that violate this policy.

To report violations, contact:

- `Codty1@outlook.xom`

All reports will be reviewed and handled in good faith.
